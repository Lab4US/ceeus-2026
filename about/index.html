SCOPE

We would like to push the boundaries of novel ultrasound applications, including but not limited to:
» BIOMED ULTRASOUND — e.g. methods/systems, LIFU, HIFU, neuromodulation, drug delivery
» WEARABLES — e.g. ultrasound wearable and implantable approaches
» MEDICAL APPLICATIONS — e.g. POCUS, clinical applications, AI and Machine Learning
» INDUSTRIAL APPLICATIONS — e.g. non-destructive testing, robotic ultrasound

Program & Tracks

Join us for a dynamic three-day symposium (June 22-24, 2026), starting at 9:00 AM each day, featuring four exciting tracks:

  1. Ultrasound Technology
  2. Wearables
  3. Medical Ultrasound
  4. Ultrasound Industrial Applications (NDT)
